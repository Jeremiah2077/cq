Privacy Notice

Version 1.0 · April 2026

At China Quest we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This notice explains when and why we collect personal information, how we use it, the conditions under which we may disclose it, and how we keep it safe.

Who are we?

China Quest is the trading name of Miles Minds Limited, a company registered in Ireland (Company No. 802673). We design and operate educational travel programmes for Irish and international schools.

Contact: info@milesminds.com · +353 87 375 2491

How do we collect information?

We collect information from you directly when you:

  • Complete our "Register Your Interest" form
  • Email us or contact us by phone
  • Interact with us at events (such as TY Show)

We may also collect technical information indirectly through cookies and analytics when you browse our website.

What type of information do we collect?

  • Email address
  • Your role (16+ student, parent/guardian, or teacher/school staff)
  • School name (optional, teachers only)
  • Consent records (timestamp and the text you agreed to)

We do not collect your name, date of birth, home address, or any special category data (health, ethnicity, religion, etc.) through this form. Providing your data is entirely voluntary.

How and why is your information used?

  • Confirm your registration via a double opt-in email
  • Send you updates about the Pioneer Programme
  • Notify you when programme registrations open
  • Route your enquiry to the appropriate communication stream

We will never use your email for unrelated marketing, and we will never sell or rent your personal data to any third party. We do not use automated decision-making or profiling.

What is our lawful basis?

We rely on consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). Consent is given freely through two separate, un-pre-checked checkboxes on our form.

  • Students aged 16+ — consent given directly
  • Students under 16 — consent given by parent/guardian (Article 8 GDPR; Section 31, Irish Data Protection Act 2018)
  • Teachers / school staff — consent given directly

You may withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before you withdrew.

16 or under

Under Irish law, the digital age of consent is 16.

  • If you are 16 or over, you may register directly.
  • If you are under 16, your parent or guardian must use the "Parent / guardian" path on our form.
  • We will not knowingly collect data from a child under 16 without parental consent.
  • All communications for under-16 registrations are sent to the parent/guardian's email.

How long do we keep your information?

  • Unconfirmed registrations — deleted after 48 hours
  • Confirmed registrations — up to 24 months
  • After 24 months with no interaction — we send a renewal email; no response within 30 days = deleted
  • Unsubscribe / consent withdrawal — deleted within 30 days

Who has access to your information?

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Email service provider (EU-hosted) — to send emails
  • Web hosting provider (EU-hosted) — to host this website

All processors act under a signed Data Processing Agreement. We do not transfer data outside the EEA.

Your rights

Under GDPR and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data
  • Restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — at any time via unsubscribe or email

To exercise any right, email info@milesminds.com. We respond within 30 days.

Keeping your information safe

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. No internet transmission is 100% secure, but we and our providers maintain high standards of security.

How to complain

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you may lodge a complaint with:
