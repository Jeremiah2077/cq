How a Tailored Programme Works

From your first call to departure day, our team handles the entire design and logistics process — all you need to do is tell us what matters to your students.

1
Discovery Call
Tell us your subjects, student ages, budget range, preferred travel dates, and any special interests or learning goals. No obligation — just a conversation.
2
Draft Itinerary
Within 5 working days we propose a complete day-by-day route — cities, activities, and curriculum connections — built around your school's priorities.
3
Refine
Up to 3 rounds of revisions at no charge. Swap cities, adjust the pace, add a service day, or change activities until the itinerary is exactly right.
4
Confirm
We quote a single fixed per-person price. A 20% deposit locks your dates — no hidden add-ons, no surprises on arrival.
5
Curriculum Mapping
We deliver a bespoke LC/JC subject mapping document tied to your chosen activities — ready to present to parents and submit with your school trip approval.
