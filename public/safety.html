Our People

The 4-on-1 Service Model

Four dedicated professionals look after your group from the moment you land to the moment you leave.

1

Curriculum Designer

Builds and oversees the programme structure, ensuring every activity maps to LC and JC specifications and delivers measurable learning outcomes.

2

Bilingual Education Mentor

Bridges language and culture on the ground, facilitating authentic interactions and helping students get the most from every encounter.

3

Safety & Logistics Officer

Manages all on-the-ground logistics, risk mitigation, and emergency protocols — keeping every day safe, smooth, and on schedule.

4

24/7 School-Family Liaison

Keeps schools and parents informed throughout the trip with daily updates and a direct line for any queries, day or night.
