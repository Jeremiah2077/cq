Terms & Conditions

Last updated: April 2026

These terms and conditions govern all programmes and services provided by Miles Minds Limited (Company No. 802673, Ireland), trading as China Quest. By booking a programme or creating an account, you agree to these terms.

Any questions should be sent to info@milesminds.com.

1. Pioneer Programme

The Pioneer Programme is a founding cohort initiative. Registration of interest and account creation for the Pioneer Programme is free of charge and requires no deposit. No financial commitment is required until a place is formally offered and accepted.

Pioneer Programme participants will be notified separately of any applicable fees, payment schedules, and cancellation terms before any commitment is made.

2. Payment

For standard programmes (Roots, Pulse, Horizon, Tailored):

  • A 10% deposit of the total programme cost is required at the time of booking to secure your place.
  • The remaining balance is due no later than 60 days before the departure date.
  • Payments are processed securely via Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards.
  • Bank transfer is available on request for group bookings.

If the balance is not received by the due date, China Quest reserves the right to treat the booking as cancelled and apply the cancellation charges set out below.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

All cancellations must be submitted in writing to info@milesminds.com. Refunds are calculated based on the date written notice is received:

  • 60+ days before departure — Full refund (100%), minus any non-recoverable third-party costs
  • 30–59 days before departure — 50% refund
  • 15–29 days before departure — 25% refund
  • 0–14 days before departure — No refund

Group bookings of 10 or more travellers may be subject to separate cancellation terms, which will be outlined in the group booking agreement.

We strongly recommend that all participants purchase comprehensive travel insurance that includes cancellation cover.

4. Alteration of Itinerary

Changes to your itinerary requested before final payment will be accommodated free of charge where possible.

After final payment, itinerary modifications may incur an administration fee of €25 per request, plus any additional costs charged by third-party suppliers (airlines, hotels, venues).

China Quest reserves the right to modify programme itineraries due to circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to weather, local regulations, venue closures, or safety concerns. In such cases, we will provide suitable alternatives of comparable quality at no additional cost.

5. Travel Documentation

It is the responsibility of each participant (and, for minors, their parent or guardian) to ensure they hold:

  • A valid passport with at least 6 months' validity beyond the return date
  • A valid Chinese visa (China Quest provides guidance and support but does not guarantee visa approval)
  • Any other documentation required for travel

China Quest accepts no liability for participants who are denied entry to China or who fail to obtain the necessary documentation.

6. Health Requirements

Participants are responsible for checking vaccination requirements and health advisories for China. China Quest recommends consulting your GP or a travel health clinic at least 6 weeks before departure.

Participants with medical conditions that may affect their ability to travel should inform China Quest in writing at the time of booking. We reserve the right to request a doctor's certificate confirming fitness to travel.

7. Insurance

All China Quest programmes include comprehensive travel insurance (TOPP-compliant) covering:

  • Medical expenses and emergency evacuation
  • Personal accident cover
  • Baggage loss or damage
  • Third-party liability

We recommend that participants also hold personal travel insurance from their home country, particularly for cancellation cover, as this is not included in the group policy.

8. Luggage Allowance

Luggage allowances are determined by the airline and class of travel. Typical economy class allowances for international flights are 23kg checked baggage and 7kg hand luggage. Internal Chinese flights typically allow 20kg checked baggage.

Excess baggage charges are the responsibility of the participant.

9. Complaints and Claims

We are committed to resolving any issues promptly. If you have a complaint:

  • Please raise it with your China Quest leader during the programme so we can address it immediately.
  • If the matter is not resolved to your satisfaction, submit a written complaint to info@milesminds.com within 30 days of your return.
  • We will acknowledge your complaint within 5 working days and provide a full response within 28 days.

10. Liability & Responsibility

China Quest acts as an organiser and coordinator of educational travel programmes. We accept responsibility for ensuring the programme is delivered as described, subject to the right to make changes as outlined in Section 4.

China Quest shall not be held liable for:

  • Loss, damage, or injury caused by the negligence of the participant
  • Disruptions caused by events beyond our control (force majeure), including natural disasters, pandemics, war, civil unrest, government actions, strikes, or transport failures
  • Loss or damage to personal belongings
  • Actions or omissions of third-party service providers (airlines, hotels, venues)

11. Safeguarding

All China Quest programmes are designed for participants aged 14–18 and operate under strict safeguarding policies:

  • All Irish leaders are Garda-vetted
  • A qualified Irish leader accompanies every group at all times
  • 24/7 emergency contact is available for parents and guardians throughout the programme
  • Daily check-in routines are maintained

12. Data Protection

We process personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

13. Governing Law

These terms and conditions are governed by the laws of Ireland. Any disputes arising shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Irish courts.

14. Changes to These Terms

China Quest reserves the right to update these terms at any time. The latest version will always be available on this page. Material changes will be communicated to affected participants by email.
