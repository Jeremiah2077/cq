Hangzhou

Where a thousand-year-old city became China's AI capital.

About Hangzhou

Song dynasty heritage meets Silicon Valley ambition

Hangzhou has been a cultural centre since the Song dynasty. Today it's home to Alibaba and one of China's most vibrant tech ecosystems. West Lake (UNESCO) sits at the city's heart — classical Chinese landscape architecture surrounded by 21st-century AI labs.

Good to Know

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March–May) and Autumn (September–November) are the best seasons. West Lake is especially beautiful during spring blossom and autumn foliage. Summers are hot and humid.

Getting There

45-minute high-speed rail from Shanghai. All internal transfers are arranged by China Quest with private coaches. Hangzhou East station is modern and well-connected.

Local Flavour

Hangzhou is famous for Longjing (Dragon Well) green tea, Dongpo pork, and West Lake vinegar fish. Tea plantation visits and local cooking experiences are included in our programmes.
