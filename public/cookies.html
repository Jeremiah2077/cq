Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how China Quest (Miles Minds Limited, Company No. 802673, Ireland) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your computer or mobile device when you visit. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (such as login status) so you don't have to re-enter them each time you visit.

What cookies do we use?

We only use strictly necessary cookies that are essential for our website to function. We do not use advertising, marketing, or tracking cookies.

Cookie Purpose Type Duration
sb-*-auth-token Maintains your login session (Supabase authentication) Strictly necessary Session / 1 year
__vercel_* Website hosting and performance (Vercel platform) Strictly necessary Session

These cookies are required for the website to work properly. They cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality such as signing in to your account.

Third-party cookies

We do not allow any third parties to place advertising or tracking cookies on our website. The only third-party services involved are:

  • Supabase (EU-hosted) — authentication and database
  • Vercel — website hosting

Neither of these services uses cookies to track you across other websites.

Do we need your consent?

Under the ePrivacy Directive and GDPR, strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent. They are exempt because the website cannot function without them.

If we add any non-essential cookies in the future (such as analytics), we will update this policy and ask for your consent before placing them.

How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may prevent you from signing in to your China Quest account.

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge — Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if we introduce new technologies or change our cookie usage. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at info@milesminds.com.
