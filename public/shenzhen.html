Shenzhen

From fishing village to the world's hardware capital in 40 years.

About Shenzhen

China's innovation engine

Shenzhen is the ultimate story of transformation. In 1980 it was a fishing village of 30,000. Today it's a megacity of 18 million and the innovation engine of global electronics. DJI drones, Huawei 5G, BGI genomics — the companies shaping the future are headquartered here.

Deng Xiaoping's Special Economic Zone experiment turned this corner of Guangdong into China's answer to Silicon Valley.

Good to Know

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers the most comfortable weather. Avoid summer months — Shenzhen's subtropical climate brings intense humidity and frequent typhoons from June to September.

Getting There

1.5-hour flight from Hangzhou. High-speed rail from Hong Kong takes just 15 minutes via the Guangzhou–Shenzhen–Hong Kong Express Rail Link. All internal transfers arranged by China Quest.

The Shenzhen Speed

A city transformed in one generation. From a fishing village of 30,000 in 1980 to a megacity of 18 million today. Deng Xiaoping's Special Economic Zone experiment — the birthplace of modern China's economic miracle.
