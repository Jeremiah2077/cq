Day by Day

Full Itinerary

Every day follows the Explore → Reflect → Challenge → Circle learning cycle. Click any day to expand the detail.

Travel Day

Dublin → Beijing
1
Dublin
Dublin → Beijing — Depart
  • Pre-departure briefing at Dublin Airport — China Quest Taskbook distributed
  • Overnight flight to Beijing (connection via European hub)
  • Pre-arrival orientation activities in the Taskbook

Beijing

Days 2–5  ·  4 Nights
2
Beijing
Arrive & Hutong Night Food Tour
  • Arrive Beijing — hotel check-in and orientation walk
  • Tiananmen Square context briefing — spatial scale, national symbolism and visual analysis
  • Evening: 3-hour guided hutong night food tour — street food, local vendors, community life
  • Evening debrief: first impressions of Beijing
Learning Focus: Modern Chinese history, food culture
3
Beijing
Forbidden City & Temple of Heaven
  • Forbidden City Treasure Hunt — gamified, curriculum-linked exploration of architecture, symbolism and imperial power
  • Temple of Heaven: geometry, cosmology and social order in Qing China
  • Tai Chi session in the temple park with local practitioners
  • Tong Ren Tang Traditional Chinese Medicine pharmacy — herbs, diagnosis and holistic healthcare philosophy
Learning Focus: LC History, Biology/Chemistry (TCM), PE
4
Beijing
Great Wall & Calligraphy
  • Jinshanling Great Wall hike (3–4 hours) — less-touristed, unrestored "wild" section with dramatic scenery
  • Engineering challenge: how was the Wall built, maintained and staffed?
  • Evening: hutong homestay dinner with a local family — home cooking, conversation, daily life
  • Calligraphy workshop with local artist — brush technique, character meaning, cultural expression
Learning Focus: Geography (defence architecture), Art (calligraphy)
5
Beijing
Summer Palace & Cultural Arts
  • Summer Palace — imperial leisure, landscape architecture and Kunming Lake
  • Student choice activity: Peking Opera mask-painting / Diabolo (Chinese yo-yo) lesson / Hanfu dress experience
  • Afternoon: hutong cycling tour — navigate Beijing's historic alley network independently
  • Evening debrief: Beijing wrap-up and Xi'an briefing
Learning Focus: History (Qing Dynasty), Art & Design

Xi'an

Days 6–8  ·  3 Nights
6
Beijing → Xi'an
High-Speed Rail & City Wall Cycling
  • Morning: check-out and transfer to Beijing South station
  • High-speed rail journey to Xi'an (approx. 4.5 hours) — China's rail infrastructure in action
  • Hotel check-in and orientation walk in the Muslim Quarter
  • Evening: sunset cycling on Xi'an City Wall (13.7 km circuit atop the ancient ramparts)
Learning Focus: Geography (military defence), transport networks
7
Xi'an
Terracotta Warriors & Shadow Puppets
  • Museum of the Terracotta Warriors — archaeological analysis, Qin dynasty, mass production and historical mystery
  • Hands-on: DIY terracotta figurine workshop — create and take home your own warrior
  • Afternoon: Xi'an Shadow Puppet Theatre — traditional performance and craft workshop
  • Evening: Tang Dynasty Dinner Show — imperial court music, dance and banquet
Learning Focus: LC History, Art (sculpture), Performing Arts
8
Xi'an
Silk Road Food & Faiths
  • Muslim Quarter guided food walk — Hui culture, Silk Road culinary heritage, street food tasting
  • Hands-on: dumpling making class with local chef (jiaozi, baozi varieties)
  • Great Mosque of Xi'an — Islamic architecture in a Chinese context; cultural fusion discussion
  • Big Wild Goose Pagoda — Tang dynasty Buddhism, Xuanzang's pilgrimage and cultural transmission
  • Evening debrief: religion, identity and the Silk Road
Learning Focus: History (Silk Road), Religious Ed, Home Economics

Shanghai

Days 9–10  ·  2 Nights
9
Xi'an → Shanghai
Yu Garden & The Bund
  • Morning: fly Xi'an → Shanghai (approx. 2.5 hours)
  • Afternoon: Yu Garden — classical Chinese garden design, Ming dynasty, Old Town bazaar
  • Evening: The Bund walking tour — colonial architecture, financial history and the Pudong skyline
  • Hotel check-in and orientation
Learning Focus: History (Treaty Ports), Geography (urbanisation)
10
Shanghai
School Exchange & French Concession
  • Morning: secondary school exchange — pre-trip homework exchanged with partner class, campus tour
  • Shared activities: cooking, sports and peer dialogue on education, culture and aspirations
  • Afternoon: French Concession walking tour — colonial legacy, art deco architecture, creative quarter
  • Evening: Shanghai programme debrief — comparing Beijing, Xi'an and Shanghai; reflective writing
Learning Focus: LC Chinese, Intercultural communication

Travel Days

Shanghai → Dublin
Closing Reflection & Depart
  • Morning: group closing reflection circle — key learning moments, changed perceptions, post-trip commitments
  • Taskbook final entry: "What will you bring home that you didn't pack?"
  • Transfer to Shanghai Pudong International Airport — depart for Dublin

Dublin
Arrive Dublin

4-on-1 Education Team
